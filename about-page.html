About Mori Idowu

I have always enjoyed travelling, am well-travelled in personal life and most importantly have a passion for travel. I am recognised for my expertise in finding your ideal holiday and can make recommendation on a variety of destinations with information about local customs, attractions, travel requirements and currencies. Whether it is a Luxury Cruise, Worldwide Holiday, Honeymoon packages, Anniversary or Event Holiday, a last Minute break or a Special deal, I will find it for you.

I have organised numerous trips in the past for family and friends, corporate and work colleagues and have always received great reviews and feedback.

I have been to numerous destinations so far but one that remains in my memory was the Velassaru Maldives, this was an ideal holiday that meets all my needs with an adventure that is worthwhile. It was awesome and literally took my breath away.  On a ridge taking in the peacefulness and taking in the vastness of it all will always stay with me.
