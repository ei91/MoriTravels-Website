About Us

At Mori Independent Travel Expert, we are dedicated to crafting personalized travel experiences that cater to your unique needs and desires. Whether you're planning an adventurous getaway, a relaxing retreat, or an immersive cultural journey, we are here to make your travel dreams a reality. We understand the importance of staying connected, which is why we offer multiple ways for you to reach us. You can contact us via email, X (formerly Twitter), or Instagram. Our team is committed to providing timely responses, and you can expect to hear back from us within 1-3 business days. Your journey begins the moment you get in touch, and we’re here to assist you every step of the way. Whether you have questions, need recommendations, or are ready to book your next adventure, don’t hesitate to reach out. Let’s explore the world together!

Frequently Asked Questions

What destinations do you offer?

We offer a wide range of destinations around the world, including popular spots and hidden gems.

How can I book a trip?

You can book a trip through our website, by calling our office, or by sending us an email.

What is your cancellation policy?

Our cancellation policy varies depending on the type of booking. Generally, cancellations made more than 30 days in advance will incur a fee, while those made closer to the departure date may be non-refundable.

Are there any age restrictions for my trips?

Most of our trips accomondate all ages, but some may have specific age requirements or recommendations. Please check the details sent about your trip after youve filled in the plan my trip form.
