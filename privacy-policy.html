Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 1st September 2024

Introduction

Welcome to Mori Travels. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy outlines what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email, phone number, and travel preferences.
  • Technical Information: IP address, browser type, and device usage data.
  • Transaction Data: Service inquiries, bookings, and payment details.
  • Feedback: Comments, reviews, and suggestions you provide.

How We Use Your Information

    • Service Delivery: To provide you with travel services, including bookings and customer support.
    • Personalization: To tailor your travel recommendations and offers.
    • Marketing: To send promotional offers or updates based on your preferences.
    • Legal Compliance: To meet legal obligations and resolve disputes if necessary.

    Security

    We take the security of your data seriously, implementing physical and electronic safeguards to ensure it is protected from unauthorized access.

    Third-Party Sharing

    Your information may be shared with trusted third-party partners involved in processing your bookings and reservations. We do not sell or trade your personal information without your consent.

    Cookies

    We use cookies to improve your browsing experience. These small files help us remember your preferences and personalize your experience during future visits.

    Your Rights

    • Access & Correction: You may request access to the information we hold about you and request corrections if needed.
    • Marketing Preferences: You can opt-out of receiving marketing emails at any time by following the instructions in the emails.
    • Withdraw Consent: If you wish to withdraw consent for processing your data, please contact us.

    Updates to This Policy

    We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the "Effective Date" will be updated accordingly.
